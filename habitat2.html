A Polar Habitat

Polar habitats are located at the very top and bottom of the world of the earth. Polar habitats are mostly cold and windy most of the time they have snow, ice and its to cold for anything to grow there.

Harp seal

Harp seals are about 5 to 6 feet long, weigh about 260 to 300 pounds, and have a robust body with a small, flat head. They have a narrow snout and eight pairs of teeth in both the upper and lower jaws. Their front flippers have thick, strong claws, while their back flippers have smaller, narrower claws.

Atlantic puffin

It is a short and stocky diving sea bird about 12 inches in length with a wingspan of 20-24 inches. It is black on its uppersides and white on its chest and belly. It has bright orange webbed feet; a white face and cheeks; and a large, triangular parrot-like bill that is bright red and yellow.
